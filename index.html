About

Hi, I'm Danny!


I like making things and meeting new people. Currently, I'm a first year student at Harvey Mudd College, where I am studying Computer Science and Mathematics with a concentration in Economics. I chose to study CS and Math because of my interest in Data Science, a multidisciplinary field that involves lots of computer science, statistics, linear algebra and calculus.


Besides learning new things, one of my all-time hobbies is adventuring into the unknown. Whether that takes place in the form of a hike (favourite!), an excursion to Antartica, or kayaking at a new location, I'm always excited to explore something new.


This exploratory nature of mine is what ties into my passion for entrepreneurship. I've attended two summer entrepreneurship programs, MIT Launch and Shad Valley, where I learned the importance of the beachead market, the customer persona, the minimum viable product, and so much more. At Launch, my team and I created Code Genius, a company that helps engineers and researchers code more effectively. Anyways, that's enough text for now so I'll stop talking :)
